What can you expect from I Am Creative Thinking?
Original drawings, paintings, graphic, and web
design all at an affordable price.

How long will it take for a project to be completed?
For drawings and paintings, they will be done within
10 business days. Graphics and web design will be
done within 5 business days, with websites of up to
5 pages.

What is the pricing for art, and web design services?
For pencil drawings, it is $65 for an 8 1/2 x 11 portrait,
with up to 3 characters. For watercolor paintings, it is
$120 for an 8 1/2 x 11 portrait with up to 3 characters.
Shipping and handling is included, with a $20 discount
for only jpeg files. All watercolor portraits will have
a matt frame. It is $250 for a website up to 5 pages for
made with HTML and CSS for one year, and a $70 yearly
maintianence fee for each aditional year.
